About the producer

The Night Owl is written by Cam, a hip-hop producer from Louisiana recording under the name Gramercy. This blog exists because too much production advice online is either too surface-level to be useful or too academic to be practical.

Everything here comes from real studio hours — the techniques that actually work, the business moves that actually pay, and the culture that shaped the sound.
