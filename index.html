About

I'm Cam — a hip-hop producer and writer from Louisiana recording under the name Gramercy. I started The Night Owl because I wanted a place that talked about hip-hop the way I experience it — the music, the culture, the business, and the craft behind the sound.

This is what I wish somebody had handed me. Real takes on what's happening in the culture, the production techniques I actually use, and the business stuff I had to learn the hard way. If you're up late thinking about music — you're already one of us.
