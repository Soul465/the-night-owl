I'm Cam — a hip-hop producer from Louisiana recording under the name Gramercy. I started The Night Owl because when I was coming up, I couldn't find production advice that was real. Everything was either too basic or too academic.

So this is what I wish somebody had handed me. The techniques I actually use, the business stuff I had to learn the hard way, and the culture that made me want to make beats in the first place. If you're in the studio late working on something — you're already one of us.
