Pitch an Article

Have an idea for a post? We're open to guest contributions and topic suggestions. If you want to pitch an article, include the following in your email:

  • A working title or topic idea
  • A brief summary of what the article would cover (2-3 sentences)
  • Which category it falls under: Production, Business, or Culture
  • Any relevant experience or links to your previous writing

We're looking for pieces that share real knowledge — practical advice, personal experience, and honest perspective. If it sounds like something a producer would actually want to read at 2 AM in the studio, it's probably a good fit.
